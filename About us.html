About Us

Allegro Musical Instruments, C.A, is the first Venezuelan company specialized in the commercialization of musical instruments. This hundred percent Venezuelan company is a leader in the market of distribution and sales of musical instruments of the most recognized brands in the world market.

Professionalism, teamwork, talent and excellence in the management of marketing and marketing have made this organization recognized nationally and internationally.

It has commercial stores served by specialized technicians in the cities of Caracas, Valencia, Punto Fijo, Barinas, Maracaibo, San Cristóbal, Maracay, Barquisimeto and soon many more.

Our Values
  • As an organization we believe in the responsibility and authenticity in the relationship with our clients.
  • We believe in transparency and honesty in the management of the company's resources.
  • We believe in teamwork and creativity to attend to new projects.

Our Mission
  • We are the leading Venezuelan company in marketing and sales of musical equipment.
  • We have the most important alliances with international companies in the sector to meet the demands of customers in Venezuela and other countries.
  • We train new generations and support music education in the new generations by sharing experiences with the most recognized people in the musical world.

Our Vision
  • At Allegro Musical Instruments, we will be recognized for the provision of professional music instrument marketing services in Venezuela and other countries.
  • We will be recognized for integrating the sale and marketing of products with a philosophy of quality care and always providing services to match our customers.
  • We will support entrepreneurs and organizations to contribute to the generation of jobs and improvement of the quality of life of people around the music market.
